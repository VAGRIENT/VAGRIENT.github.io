FAQ

Why does the Armlys Clan have no arms?

The Armlys have no arms due to the species' evolution of never requiring such limbs as they instead use a type of telekinetic force to handle and manage nearby objects when required.

 

Who is the developer of VAGRIENT?

VAGRIENT is currently being solo developed by Andrew "VAGRIENT" Bray in Australia.

 

When is it being released and where will I be able to purchase the game?

The game is scheduled to be released on 21st March 2025 and will be available on the Steam Store.

 

What platforms/OS will it be available for?

The game is being developed for PC/Windows on Steam with potential future plans to expand to other platforms/OS.

 

Can I feature your upcoming game?

You are welcome to create content, such as articles, YouTube videos etc. featuring VAGRIENT content.

 

How did you come up with the title and name for the main character?

It was created by combining two relevent words into one. The two words are "vagrant" and "alien".
