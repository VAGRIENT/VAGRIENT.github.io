About This Game

Play as an Armlys alien named Vagrient in this evolving sci-fi action/adventure game, featuring RPG elements. It's full of exploration, combat, character progression, puzzle solving and obstacle clearing!

A large, on-going passion project being crafted with love and the desire to produce uniqueness.

 

Features

Discover and use Warpgates to travel from one planet to another

Explore a vast solar system of planets containing what Vagrient desires

Engage in combat with other entities whilst managing energy output and consumption

Fight the unique Titan bosses, learn and strategise against their moves

Tackle any puzzles and obstacles that get in the way of the main objective

Learn new skills, allowing for different movement and combat techniques

Find old Armlys Relics, revitalise them and harness their powers

Use the interactive "Sentinel Link" tool provided by Sentinel

Extra content unlocked after game completion, utilising the Steam Leaderboards feature

Plus lots of other exciting content to uncover and experience
