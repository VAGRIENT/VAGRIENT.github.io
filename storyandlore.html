STORY AND LORE

Vagrient

An elite soldier of the Armlys. His combat skills and intelligence surpasses the rest of the clan, Vagrient is considered the alpha!

Stranded In Another Solar System

The Armlys Clan faces war against a foreign enemy. The worst is feared and Vagrient's due return has come to a sudden halt. The warpgate's connection that links the distant solar system where he is currently located and his home system has been corrupted. Restoring power to the connection would again allow for warp travel however, it comes at a cost. It requires a substantial amount of an extremely rare energy source. Vagrient has only one clear option and that is to search for the energy. The planet that he is stranded on does contain other active gates, connecting local planets of the solar system. This entire system has been considered uncharted territory by his clan for many years other than the routine visits that occur to the planet he currently stands on. Returning home is top priority but the tasks at hand are unknown...

Life In The Universe

Known lore on some of the hostiles Vagrient will face on different planets. The remaining is left for you to discover.

Slasher

Pawns of the colony. They attack with their long sharp claws. While weak as an individual, there's strength in numbers.

Ptera

Their job is to spread larvae across the land. Each larva grows to become a Slasher. The other role is to protect the queen.

Slime

Slime grows from the inside of the trees. Lumps detatch themselves to become single beings.

Mucusman

If enough spores in the environment air absorb into a Slime, it will evolve in to this atrocity.

Zealot

Wielding weapons crafted by the crystal extracted from the land. They are loyal to their deity.
