TRADING CARD GAME

VAGRIENT TCG

A VAGRIENT trading card game is in the works. It's currently in an early trial period of it's creation, however to kick things of Andrew has released an extremely limited promo holographic Sentinel card. Only 10 copies of the card will ever be in circulation, worldwide! They are the first VAGRIENT cards ever created and mark the beginning of the TCG. Continue reading to find out how you can get a chance at acquiring one.

The distribution of the 10 promo cards is being handled exclusively by Mighty Toys Collectibles!
