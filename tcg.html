TRADING CARD GAME

VAGRIENT TCG

A VAGRIENT trading card game is in the works. It's currently in the early period of it's creation, however to kick things of Andrew is soon releasing an extremely limited promo holographic card. Only 10 copies of the card will be released and in circulation, world wide! It will be the first VAGRIENT cards ever created and will mark the beginning of the TCG.

How the distribution of the 10 promo cards is being handled, is yet to be determined!
