About This Game

Play as an Armlys alien named Vagrient in this sci-fi action/adventure game full of exploration, combat, character progression, puzzle solving and obstacle clearing!

 

Features

Discover and use warpgates to travel from one planet to another

Explore a vast solar system of planets potentially containing what Vagrient desires

Engage in combat with other entities whilst managing energy output and consumption

Fight unique bosses, learn and strategize against there moves

Tackle any puzzles and obstacles that get in the way of the main objective

Learn new skills on the journey, allowing for different movement and combat techniques

Plus lots of other exciting content to uncover and experience
